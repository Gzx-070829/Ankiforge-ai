主动回忆为什么有效？Why does active recall work?

主动回忆要求学习者在不查看答案时从记忆中提取信息。提取本身会强化记忆路径，也能暴露知识缺口。间隔复习把提取安排在即将遗忘之前，从而提高长期保持。Active recall asks learners to retrieve information without looking at the answer. Retrieval strengthens access paths and reveals gaps. Spaced review schedules retrieval before forgetting, improving long-term retention.
