# Privacy

AnkiForge AI runs locally as an Anki add-on.

When you actively click to generate cards, the current study material is sent
to the AI provider you configured so that the provider can generate card
drafts. Your API key is used only for the current session and is not saved by
AnkiForge AI.

AnkiForge AI does not proactively collect telemetry. Cards are written to Anki
only after you review and confirm the write operation.

You are responsible for reviewing the privacy policy and data-handling terms
of the AI provider you choose before sending study material to it.
